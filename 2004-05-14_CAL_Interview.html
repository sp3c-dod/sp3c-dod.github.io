CAL Main Interview - 5/14/04

As appeared on Got Frag?:
Sp3cIaLisT #sp3c

sp3cIaLisT is a fairly new, post-1.0 clan which has really managed to do well lately. This clan has went through many changes which some could consider major, but it doesn't seem to phase them whatsoever. I spoke with the Sp3c leader BuLLeT about how the clan has been doing and what we can expect from them this season.

Interview with Sp3c-BuLLeT
gratt - Sp3c is a relatively new clan in DoD, but have already managed to prove themselves more then once, even with what some may consider to be "devastating" roster changes. With this said, how do you feel you played last season?
Sp3c-BuLLeT - Last season we had a lot of problems with our starters not showing up for matches and if they did show up they were really unprepared for the match. At the end of the season I recruited more active people that planned on showing up, so now I believe our roster is actually better, because what good are great players if they don't show up.

gratt - What do you have planned for the new season? Is there anything specific you would like to accomplish or work on as a team?
Sp3c-BuLLeT - We need to work on better communication. Most of talking on vent is useless chatter we have to curb that only talk about useful things.

gratt - How do you feel about the introduction of CAL-im? Will it help to make the competition in main this season better or worse?
Sp3c-BuLLeT - I personally don't feel IM is needed, but it will help keep some of the CAL-O caliber teams out of Main which will make our main matches more intense.

gratt - Are there any teams which you are specifically worried about playing?
Sp3c-BuLLeT - I feel if we practice and play hard every wed. we are capable of beating any team in CAL-M. I look it at as it is ours to win or lose (or DOD's to lose for us when our guys get disconnected mid-game).
